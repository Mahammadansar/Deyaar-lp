FAQ's

About Deyaar

Where are Deyaar's key projects located?

Deyaar has developments in Business Bay, Al Barsha, Jumeirah Village Circle (JVC), Dubai Silicon Oasis, and Dubai Production City.

Is Deyaar a publicly listed company?

Yes, Deyaar is publicly listed on the Dubai Financial Market (DFM) and is one of the UAE's leading real estate players.

Are Deyaar properties freehold?

Most Deyaar developments in freehold areas offer full ownership for both UAE nationals and international investors.

Does Deyaar offer post-handover payment plans?

Yes, Deyaar often introduces flexible post-handover plans, especially for its off-plan developments.

What is Deyaar's most recognized project?

Regalia in Business Bay is among Deyaar's flagship developments, featuring smart home integration and wellness-inspired amenities.

Are Deyaar properties good for rental income?

Yes. Deyaar's locations are in high-demand areas and offer competitive rental yields and high occupancy.

Does Deyaar offer property management services?

Yes. Deyaar also manages properties through its dedicated Property Management division, making it easier for landlords and investors.

How can I buy a Deyaar property through Indus?
